---
title: "The floating bubble"
description: "Access your snippets from a draggable overlay that stays on top of everything"
---

The floating bubble is a persistent circular button that lives in the corner of your screen. It lets you search and copy snippets without leaving the page you're working on — no context switching required.

## Open and close the panel

Click the bubble to expand the quick-access panel. The panel contains:

- A **search bar** that auto-focuses when the panel opens.
- A **scrollable list** of your snippets with one-click copy buttons.

To close the panel, click the bubble again, click **Close Panel**, or click the minimize button inside the panel.

<Tip>
  The floating bubble is especially useful when you're working in another application or browser tab. Keep the bubble visible and click it to grab a snippet without losing your place.
</Tip>

## Copy a snippet from the panel

Click any snippet in the list to copy its code to the clipboard instantly. No confirmation step required.

## Hover preview

Hover over any snippet in the panel to see a mini syntax-highlighted preview of the first 5 lines of code. This lets you confirm you're copying the right snippet before clicking.

## Move the bubble

Click and hold the bubble, then drag it to any position on the screen. Release to drop it in place. The bubble starts in the bottom-right corner (20px from each edge) and remembers its position within the session.

Drag also works on touch devices — press and hold the bubble, then drag with your finger.

## Search inside the panel

<Note>
  The bubble's search bar uses the same search logic as the main snippet list — it matches against title, code, language, and tags simultaneously.
</Note>

Type in the search bar inside the panel to filter the snippet list in real time. The results update as you type.

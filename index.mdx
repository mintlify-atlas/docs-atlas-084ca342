## What is Snippet Bubble Manager?

Snippet Bubble Manager is a **floating, always-on-top snippet tool** that lives inside your browser workflow. It gives you instant one-click access to your personal code library without switching windows or digging through files.

Sign in with Google, save your snippets to Firestore, and the draggable bubble sits quietly in the corner of any page — ready to copy code to your clipboard the moment you need it.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with Google">
    Firebase Authentication handles sign-in. Your Google account becomes the owner of all your snippets — no other user can read or modify them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create snippets">
    Add snippets manually in the Monaco editor, generate them with AI, or apply one of the built-in templates. Each snippet has a title, code, language, optional description, tags, and folder assignment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the bubble">
    The floating bubble sits in the corner of your screen. Click it to expand the quick panel, search your snippets, and copy code to your clipboard in one click.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay organized">
    Group related snippets into folders. Pin the most-used ones to the top. Star your favorites for quick filtering.
  </Step>
</Steps>
